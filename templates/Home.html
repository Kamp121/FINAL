    Our mission at flourish is to enrich and employ those who are less fortunate in our communities and give back to its citizens. Homelessness is an issue that needs to be addressed, it’s our job and responsibility to give them the same opportunity we have been given. It’s not just enough to have homeless shelters and food banks, we need to equip them with the skills they need to thrive in the workforce. We plan to contribute to the aid of combating homelessness by providing homeless people our program for whatever amount of time is necessary to get them the help they need. Then we offer our two option initiatives, allowing them to take the business and life skills they have learned and use them to benefit in our organization or take up work elsewhere. Allowing them to have the opportunity to learn job skills that benefit them in the workforce. By providing customers with a service to help all of their needs we can bring in money that is split between the company and employee.
